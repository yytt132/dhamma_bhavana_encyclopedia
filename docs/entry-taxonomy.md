# Entry Taxonomy

The encyclopedia uses multiple entry types.

## `concept`

A major philosophical, doctrinal, or contemplative concept suitable for a long essay.

Examples:

- `bhavana`
- `sunyata`
- `anatman`
- `dependent-origination`

## `practice`

A concrete cultivated activity, method, or training mode.

Examples:

- `anapanasati`
- `zazen`
- `tonglen`
- `pranayama`

## `lexical`

A shorter dictionary-style term entry.

Examples:

- `sati`
- `smrti`
- `samadhi`
- `dhyana`

## `tradition`

A tradition, school, lineage, or modern program.

Examples:

- `theravada`
- `madhyamaka`
- `soto-zen`
- `mbsr`

## `comparison`

A comparative essay or matrix.

Examples:

- `samatha-vs-vipassana`
- `anapanasati-vs-pranayama`
- `mantra-japa-dhikr-jesus-prayer`

## `source`

A bibliographic or textual source page.

Examples:

- canonical text
- commentary
- modern monograph
- dictionary
- local corpus aggregate
