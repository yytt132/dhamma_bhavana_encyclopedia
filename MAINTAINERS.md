# Maintainers

## Current Maintainer

- GitHub: `yytt132`
- Role: founding maintainer and project lead

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Keep the repository public and usable.
- Review pull requests.
- Triage issues.
- Maintain schemas and validation tools.
- Protect the repository from copyright-risk material.
- Coordinate editorial and subject review.
- Publish releases and roadmap updates.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Future maintainers should demonstrate sustained, careful contribution in at least one area:

- Editorial review.
- Source metadata.
- Buddhist studies or contemplative practice expertise.
- Korean-language writing or translation.
- Website/tooling maintenance.
- Schema and data modeling.
