# Code of Conduct

This project is dedicated to careful, respectful, and source-aware work.

Contributors are expected to:

- Be kind and precise.
- Welcome correction.
- Avoid sectarian polemics.
- Distinguish scholarship, tradition, and personal practice.
- Respect source communities and living lineages.
- Follow the copyright policy.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, bad-faith editing, or knowingly adding copyrighted material that cannot be publicly redistributed.

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect the integrity and safety of the project.
